Systems Engineer & Project Engineer

Ideal Job

The ideal role I will be aiming for is the Systems Engineer/Project Engineer. The position allows you to have the most significant input into developing, building, and maintaining a software environment for any business, whether it is a new startup or an existing multinational corporation. You are given a problem from a client, and you have to come up with a solution that fits the best. This role's appeal is that you can be at the ground level of a whole new development or come into an existing environment and put your personal touches on it.

Required skills, qualifications, and experience

The skills that you need for this role range from technical, logical, and personal. You have to understand the software and the environment and how it operates. The technical skills could become very niche, also with new technologies emerging all the time. You have to have a systems thinking and critical thinking background which comes into the logical skill. The client will have a complex problem, and you will need to provide them with a simple solution and explain it to them. This is where personal skills will come into play. You will need to be able to take all that technical information and translate it so that a non-technical minded person will understand what you are saying.

Qualifications and certificates on Windows Server, Linux, specific software, will all be needed; having a Bachelors on Information Technology would put you in a more favourable position.

The experience will range from project to project, but having at least 5+ years of doing similar administration types would be required for my expectations.
